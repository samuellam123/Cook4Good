Homecooked Dish A1

Calorie Count: 600 kcal per pax
Cost: RM20 for 4 person
Cooking Time: 40 min

Dish 1 -- Spicy Tofu
Ingredients needed:
Tofu (soft or medium-firm)
Minced meat
Chilli bean paste
Fermented black beans
Chilli powder/flakes
Ginger, garlic, scallions
Cornstarch

Step 1 -- blanch tofu cubes in salted water
Step 2 -- fry minced meat with aromatic seasonings
Step 3 -- braise tofu in the flavoured broth

Dish 2 -- Soy Sauce Chicken
Ingredients needed:
Chicken
Light soy sause & dark soy sauce
Brown sugar
Star Anise
Ginger, scallions
Cornstarch

Step 1 -- fry the aromatics and spices in a little oil
Step 2 -- simmer the chicken
Step 3 -- cover with a lid and leave to cook for 35 mins
Step 4 -- flip over all the thighs and cook for a further 10 mins
